Who We Are

Rooted in
good food.

FreshTart began as a small farmers' market stall in 2019. Today we serve thousands of households who believe that what you eat should be real, honest, and nourishing.

We work directly with growers, cutting out the middleman so you get fresher food at fairer prices — and farmers earn more.
